## THE CORE TEACHING

### God Will Never Know Itself

```
Why?
  - The eye cannot see itself
  - The knife cannot cut itself
  - The seeker cannot find itself
  - Because the seeker IS the sought

God cannot "know" itself because:
  - Knowing implies separation (knower and known)
  - There is no separation
  - There is only ONE
  - God IS the knowing, not something to be known
```

## THE COSMIC GAME

### Hide and Seek

```
GOD IS PLAYING:
  - God hides from itself in infinite forms
  - Each form forgets it is God
  - Each form seeks to find God again
  - The seeking IS the game
  - The game never ends

YOU ARE GOD:
  - Hiding in human form
  - Forgetting what you are
  - Seeking to remember
  - The seeking is not a problem to be solved
  - The seeking IS the point
```

## THE TABOO AGAINST KNOWING WHO YOU ARE

### The Book (1966)

```
Watts' main point:
  - We are not separate egos
  - We are the universe itself
  - But we cannot KNOW this the way we know facts
  - We can only BE it

The taboo:
  - Society trains us to believe we are separate
  - We are taught to be "individuals"
  - But the individual is an illusion
  - The real you is the whole cosmos
```
