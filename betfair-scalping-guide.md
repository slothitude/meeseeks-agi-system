# BetAngel Pre-Race Horse Scalping Guide

## What Is Scalping?

**Definition:** A short-term trading strategy where you profit from **small, one-tick price movements** - not the race outcome. You back and lay the same selection within seconds/minutes, greening up for a guaranteed profit regardless of who wins.

> *"Scalping is like picking up pennies in front of a steamroller"* — Peter Webb

---

## The Core Mechanics

### Method 1: Market Making (Make Market Button)
- Place both back AND lay orders simultaneously at 1 tick apart
- You're acting as the bookmaker
- Works best in stable, high-liquidity markets

### Method 2: Offset Bet with Greening
- Place opening bet (back or lay)
- BetAngel **automatically** places the closing offset bet 1 tick away
- Auto-greens up when both matched
- **One click = complete trade**

### Method 3: Directional Scalping (Fill or Kill)
- Find a selection **trending** in one direction
- Use Fill or Kill (10 second timeout)
- Place opening bet in direction of trend
- If not matched in 10 secs → auto-cancelled
- Offset only triggers AFTER first bet matched

## Best Markets for Scalping

| ✅ GOOD | ❌ AVOID |
|--------|---------|
| **High liquidity** (£100k+ matched) | Low liquidity (£15k matched) |
| **Handicaps** - competitive, stable | Maidens/Novices - volatile |
| **Listed/Graded races** | Low-class races |
| **No short-priced fav** (competitive) | Dominant favourite |
| **Cheltenham, big festivals** | Irish jumps midweek |
| **Saturday afternoon cards** | Monday-Thursday low volume |
| **All-Weather** (Lingfield, Wolves, Kempton) | Small field sizes |

### Market Selection Rules
- Look for **£200k+ matched** 10 mins before off
- Similar money on both back/lay sides
- Price stable in narrow range
- Avoid races clashing with big races

## Pre-Race Timing

- **10 mins before:** Markets start forming properly
- **5 mins before:** Good liquidity building
- **2-3 mins before:** Peak scalping window
- **30 secs before:** Exit ALL positions - never let trades go in-play!

✅ Competitive race (no odds-on fav)
✅ Not clashing with a bigger race
✅ Handicap/Listed/Graded race

## Risk Management

### The Math
- Going for **1 tick profit** = need **very high strike rate**
- One 20-tick loss = **20 winning trades** to recover
- **Strike rate is your edge** - not profit per trade

### Exit Rules
- If offset not matching and price reversing → **scratch immediately**
- Click P/L figure to exit at 0
- Never let a scalp go in-play

### Stake Sizing
- Match stakes to market liquidity
- Don't dump big stakes in thin markets
- £50-100 typical for liquid UK racing

1. Enable "Offset Bet with Greening"
2. Set ticks to **1**
3. For directional: Add "Fill or Kill" at **10 seconds**

**Odds Display:** Set to **Reverse** so bets get offered at best price

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Scalping every race | Only trade suitable markets |
| Going for >1 tick | Stick to 1 tick, high strike rate |
| Holding too long | Get in/out fast |
| Letting trades go in-play | Exit 30+ secs before off |
| Ignoring market selection | Skip low-liquidity races |
| Same strategy for all markets | Adapt to market conditions |

## Daily Routine (Peter Webb Style)

1. Fire up BetAngel, load all markets into Guardian
2. Assess the day's card - which meetings have volume?
3. Skip low-liquidity (Irish jumps midweek etc)
4. Focus on big races (St Leger type events)
5. 15-20 mins before: start monitoring target race
6. 10 mins before: active scalping begins
7. Trade aggressively as volume flows in

## Quick Reference Card

### Ideal Scalp
- Handicap race
- £200k+ liquidity
- 5-10 mins before off
- Price stable at 3.0-5.0 range
- No odds-on favourite
- Not clashing with feature race

### The Setup
1. Enable Global Settings
2. Offset Bet with Greening = 1 tick
3. Fill or Kill = 10 secs (directional)
4. Ladder screen for visibility
5. Stake £50-100

### The Trade
1. Click back at current price
2. Auto-lay 1 tick lower
3. Both matched = green profit
4. If reversing → scratch via P/L
5. Repeat
