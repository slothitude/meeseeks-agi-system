# Betfair Study Summary - Key Insights

## The 65/35 Rule
**Winning = 35% selections + 65% staking habits**

Most punters fail not because of bad selections, but because of:
- Betting too much relative to bank
- No staking plan
- Chasing losses
- Impatience

---

## Professional Staking Method

**Bet to COLLECT 4-5% of your bank**

Examples ($10,000 bank):
- $2.00 odds → stake $200 (collect $400)
- $4.00 odds → stake $100 (collect $400)
- $8.00 odds → stake $50 (collect $400)

**Why it works:**
- Proportional to winning chance
- Survives losing runs
- Maximises compound growth
- Comfortable bet sizes

---

## Place Betting = The Slow Bleed

**Reality Check:**
- Win edge of +20% → Place edge of only +6.7%
- More collects, but inferior dividends
- Bank slowly deteriorates

**Only place bet when:**
1. Exceptional scenario (dominant fav drawn awkward)
2. Big market mistake on longer priced horse
3. Very confident in massive edge

**Better strategy:** Focus on shorter end of win market for more regular collects.

## Misleading Statistics (IGNORE THESE)

### 1. Win Strike Rate
**Lower is better!**
- 0% strike rate: +48.8% POT
- >30% strike rate: -7.5% POT
- Market overvalues high strike rates

### 2. First Up Record
**Never won first up = better!**
- Never won first up: +3.6% POT
- Has won first up: -7.6% POT
- Market overvalues previous first up wins

### 3. Distance Wins
**Hasn't won at distance = better!**
- Never won at distance: -6.1% POT
- Has won at distance: -7.2% POT
- Market overvalues obvious form

### 4. Barrier Draws (Sprints)
**Wide barriers = better!**
- Barriers 1-3: -6.2% POT
- Barriers 10+: -2.9% POT
- Market overvalues inside draws

**Exception:** Staying races (2000m+) - wide draws are worse

### DON'T Value:

## Creating Your Own Prices

**Step 1:** Rank horses
**Step 2:** Assign lengths from top
**Step 3:** Apply score (2 points per length)
**Step 4:** Calculate percentage (score / total)
**Step 5:** Convert to price (100 / percentage)

**Add Context Tags:**
- **SHORT:** Couldn't price any shorter (conservative)
- **LONG:** Couldn't price any longer (optimistic)
- **FLAT:** Minimal uncertainty (balanced)
- **QUERY:** High guesswork (market is better guide)

## Scalping Strategy

**Steam Detection:**
- Watch for 5%+ price drops in 5-10 mins
- Back at peak price
- Lay when price settles
- Target 1-2 tick profit

**AU Market Behavior:**
- Lower liquidity = slower matching
- Tighter spreads = smaller profits
- Automation essential
- Patience required
