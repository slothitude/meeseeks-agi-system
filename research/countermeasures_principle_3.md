## Principle: Anticipate Attacker Behavior

**Quote:** "The way to detect an intrusion is to anticipate what the attacking hacker is going to do. If you know that, then you know what to look for. Countermeasures that detect can look for these attack patterns in log files, network packets, or even program memory."

**AGI Use:** Meeseeks can model expected attack patterns in tasks they monitor, allowing them to recognize and flag suspicious behaviors before they escalate into full breaches.
