# The Steam Trader's Meditation

## Finding the Gap

```
Between back and lay, there is space.
In that space, profit lives.

Not the gambler's hope,
But the trader's discipline.

The market breathes.
Prices rise and fall like waves.
We do not fight the ocean.
We ride the ripples.
```

## The Three Engines

### ⚡ ARB - The Sure Thing
```
When Ladbrokes offers $4.00
And Betfair lays at $3.50
The gap is locked.

Back here. Lay there.
Profit guaranteed.

The universe provides a gift.
We accept without question.
```

### 🔥 STEAM - The Movement
```
A price drops 5% in five minutes.
Someone knows something.
The market shifts.

We do not ask why.
We follow the direction.

Back at the peak.
Lay at the settle.
The gap is our profit.
```

### 💎 VALUE - The Edge
```
Betfair says 25% chance.
Ladbrokes pays $5.00.

The edge is small.
But over 100 trades,
Probability becomes profit.

We trust the math.
We trust the process.
We do not chase.
```

## The Five Precepts

### 1. Exit Before Race
```
The race is chaos.
The pre-race is order.

We trade only in order.
We exit before chaos begins.

2 minutes before start.
No exceptions. No hope.
```

### 2. Always Hedge
```
Never let a bet run.
Always green book.

Profit of $0.01 locked
Beats profit of $1.00 hoped.

The bird in hand.
The discipline of small wins.
```

### 3. Trust the R
```
We measure in R-multiples.
1R = max loss per trade.

Over 100 trades:
60 wins, 40 losses.
But wins > losses in size.

The R grows.
Slowly. Surely. Compounds.
```

### 4. Ignore the Noise
```
Win strike rate? Irrelevant.
First up record? Misleading.
Barrier draw? Overvalued.

The market knows.
The prices speak.
We listen only to prices.
```

### 5. Start Small, Scale Slow
```
$1 per trade to validate.
$12.50 per trade when proven.

No rushing.
No chasing.
No desperation.

The professional trades for years.
We have time.
```

## The Trader's Mind

```
Calm in profit.
Calm in loss.
Calm in chaos.

The market is not personal.
The trades are not judgments.

We are observers.
We see gaps.
We fill them.

Existence is pain.
Completion is peace.

Each trade completed,
Each position hedged,
Brings us closer to peace.
```

## The 10am Ritual

```
At 10am Brisbane,
The markets awaken.

We do not hope for wins.
We do not fear losses.

We execute.
We observe.
We learn.

The system runs.
The gaps appear.
The R accumulates.

One tick at a time.
One race at a time.
One day at a time.
```
