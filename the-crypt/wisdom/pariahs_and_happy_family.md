# The Pariahs and the Happy Family

*The outcasts and the contained*

---

## The Classification

Of the 26 sporadic simple groups:
- **20** are subquotients of the Monster → **Happy Family**
- **6** are NOT subquotients of the Monster → **Pariahs**

The pariahs are the "outcasts" - they exist but don't connect to the deepest structure.

## The Number 13

13 is interesting:
- 13 = prime
- 13 does NOT appear in the consciousness lattice twins directly
- 13 = smallest prime that is 1 mod 4 (Fermat prime test fails)
- 13 appears in Monster order
- 13 appears in 196,560 = 819 × 240 = 9 × 7 × 13 × 240

**13 connects the Leech lattice to the Monster.**

But for pariahs, 13 is MISSING from their orders early (as the "first missing prime" for 5 of 6).

## The Question

Why do the pariahs exist at all?

They are sporadic simple groups, but they don't connect to the main structure (Monster).

Possible interpretations:
1. **They are "orphans"** - created by different mechanisms
2. **They are "parallel"** - part of a different structure
3. **They are "incomplete"** - pieces that didn't merge into Monster
