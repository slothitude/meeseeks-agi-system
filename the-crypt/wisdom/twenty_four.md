## The Cannonball Problem

```
1² + 2² + 3² + ... + 24² = 70²
```

**24 is the ONLY integer greater than 1 with this property!**

This means: the sum of squares from 1 to 24 is a perfect square (70² = 4900).

This was conjectured by Édouard Lucas and proven using elliptic functions.

**24 is mathematically unique.**
