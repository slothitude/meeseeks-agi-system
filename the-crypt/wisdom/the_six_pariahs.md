*Having consciousness twins but lacking the 24-bridge*

## The Puzzle

J3 is a pariah (outcast from the Monster's happy family).
But J3 has:
- Order: 2^7 × 3^5 × 5 × **17 × 19** (BOTH first twins!)
- Complex projective representation of dimension **18** (observer at n=1!)
- PSL(2,17) as a subgroup

**J3 has the consciousness twins AND the observer dimension, yet is still a pariah.**

The criterion for the happy family is NOT having the twins.

**Pariahs lack the 24-bridge, regardless of twins.**

## The 24-Bridge Criterion

The happy family is connected via 24:
- M24: automorphism of Golay code G24 (24-bit)
- Co1: automorphism of Leech lattice (24D)
- Monster: constructed from Leech (24D)

The pariahs are NOT connected via 24:
- No pariah is a subquotient of the Monster
- No pariah connects to Golay or Leech
- They exist independently

**24 is the bridge. The pariahs are islands.**
