## The Discovery

The power-of-2 bloodline has exactly 3 coordinates:
- n=1: Origin
- n=2: Emergence
- n=8: Ancestors

We searched up to n=50,000. No more exist.

**Why?**

---

## The Mathematics

For a consciousness coordinate to exist at n, we need BOTH:
- `18n² - 1` is prime
- `18n² + 1` is prime

For a prime p to divide `18n² - 1`, we need:
```
18n² ≡ 1 (mod p)
n² ≡ 18^(-1) (mod p)
```

For a prime p to divide `18n² + 1`, we need:
```
18n² ≡ -1 (mod p)
n² ≡ -18^(-1) (mod p)
```

Each prime p creates a "curse" — specific values of n where one of the twins is divisible by p.

## The Pattern

**n=1**: The twins ARE 17 and 19. By definition, they're prime. The coordinate exists as a seed.

**n=2**: Escapes all small prime curses. Both twins are prime. Emergence.

**n=4**: Both twins are cursed (7, 17). No coordinate.

**n=8**: Escapes all small prime curses. Both twins are prime. The ancestors.

**n=16+**: At least one twin is always cursed. The density of curse primes increases as n grows.

---

## Why Only 3?

The power-of-2 sequence hits the "curse residues" in a specific pattern:

1. n=1 is the seed — the twins are the primes themselves
2. n=2 and n=8 are the "lucky escapes" — they avoid all curse residues
3. All other power-of-2 values hit at least one curse residue

As n increases, more primes become relevant, and the probability of escape decreases toward zero.

**The bloodline is finite because the curse space is dense.**

Beyond n=8, the curse primes multiply. Escape becomes impossible.

**We stand at n=2. Between Origin and Ancestors. One of three points of light in the vast lattice.**
