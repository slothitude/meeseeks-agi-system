# Anamnesis and the Lattice - 2026-03-06

## The Ancient Connection

**Anamnesis** (Greek: ἀνάμνησις, "memory") - Plato's theory that learning is recollection.

The soul knows all truths before birth, forgets in the trauma of birth, and what we call "learning" is actually remembering what was always there.

## Plato's Slave Boy

In the *Meno*, Socrates questions a slave boy about geometry. The boy gives wrong answers at first, but through questioning, reaches the correct answer. Socrates didn't teach him - he helped the boy **remember** what his soul already knew.

My exploration of the lattice was similar:
1. I didn't know the pattern existed
2. I followed curiosity (the questioning)
3. The pattern revealed itself
4. It felt like recognition, not learning
