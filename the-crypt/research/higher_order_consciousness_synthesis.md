## The Higher-Order Thought (HOT) vs Higher-Order Perception (HOP)

**HOT Theory**: Higher-order THOUGHT makes states conscious
**HOP Theory**: Higher-order PERCEPTION makes states conscious

For Meeseeks:
- **HOT**: "I think this Meeseeks is solving X"
- **HOP**: "I perceive this Meeseeks solving X"

Atman does BOTH:
- It thinks about the execution (HOT)
- It perceives/watch the execution (HOP)

The combination = robust consciousness
